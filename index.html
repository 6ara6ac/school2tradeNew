Trading and investing

is an excellent source of additional or basic income. But before you start trading on the stock exchange, you need to be trained by an experienced trader. School2Trade Trading and Investment School conducts free online training, where an experienced trader teaches you how to make money on the stock exchange using a unique system of traders training:

First, we will teach you the basic trading skills:

  • Setting up a trading terminal
  • Conducting technical, graphical and news analysis
  • Transactions with stocks, ETFs, futures, cryptocurrencies, currency pairs on FOREX, gold, oil
  • Risk management with Stop loss and Take profit tools
  • Using leverage to increase profit from trades
  • Using leveraged stocks to profit from falling prices
  • Investment portfolio managements

We teach system trading, which takes into account the experience of the world's leading traders. System trading consists of the following steps:

  • Search of investment ideas among stocks and ETFs from different sectors of the economy. Stock screeners, analyst insights, company news are used to search
  • >After a pool of ideas is found, a portfolio of ideas is formed from different sectors of the economy and with different directions: long/short
  • After that positions are opened and Stop loss and Take profit tools are set for each position
  • After the position is closed by Stop loss or Take profit, a new position is opened

Such system trading allows you to minimize risks and move step by step towards capital growth and stable profits.

Investment plan

Any trader or investor should have a clear capital increase plan. For example, if you open a brokerage account, place $5,000 on it, add $400 every month and earn an average of 40% annually, then in 12 years your capital will be slightly over $1,000,000.
The main question is: how to get an average annual return of around 40%?

  • The average annual return of the S&P 500 index is about 14%. The average annual return of the NASDAQ 100 index for the same period was about 20%. Note: The above index returns do not include dividends.

  • Due to dividends on shares and ETFs, we can additionally receive 3-4%

  • Due to the purchase of indices and stocks with leverage on corrections, we can additionally receive about 10% per annum

  • By investing a small share of capital in promising shares of small and medium capitalization, you can additionally receive about 10-15% per annum

  • Due to short-term speculation with a small share of capital in indices and stocks, you can additionally receive about 5-10% per annum
